PRIVACY POLICY

DROP YOUR PHONE

Last updated: May 2026

Drop Your Phone is a social justice art project and Chrome browser extension about surveillance, data brokers, and immigration enforcement. This policy explains what data the extension handles and how.

Data We Collect

If you use the opt-out feature, you may voluntarily enter:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • City and state

This information is stored exclusively on your own device using Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local). It is never transmitted to any server operated by this extension, never shared with third parties, and never used for any purpose other than pre-filling opt-out forms on data broker websites at your direction.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect browsing history
  • We do not collect analytics or usage statistics
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels
  • We do not transmit any information to external servers
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize any data in any form

What Happens When You Opt Out

When you click "Erase Me From All Of Them," the extension opens browser tabs to data broker opt-out pages and pre-fills the forms with the information you entered. Your information is sent directly from your browser to those data broker websites — the same as if you had filled the forms yourself manually. The extension does not intercept or log this submission.

The privacy practices of those individual data broker websites are governed by their own privacy policies, not this one.

Tracker Blocking

The extension automatically dismisses cookie consent banners on websites you visit. It reads page content to detect these banners and clicks the most privacy-preserving option available ("necessary only" or "reject all"). No page content is recorded or transmitted.

The Game

The included browser game ("Signal") runs entirely locally in your browser. It does not connect to any network, collect any information, or interact with any external service.

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab — to interact with the current tab for cookie banner dismissal
  • alarms — to guarantee opt-out tabs close within a fixed time even if a site redirects or shows a CAPTCHA, so the queue never stalls
  • scripting — to inject the cookie banner blocker and opt-out form filler
  • storage — to save your opt-out profile and settings locally on your device
  • tabs — to open data broker opt-out pages in background tabs
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) — required to dismiss cookie banners on any website you visit

Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by this extension: open Chrome, go to chrome://extensions, find Drop Your Phone, click "Details," then "Extension options" or clear site data. Alternatively, uninstalling the extension removes all stored data immediately.

Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
